In Depth: PARAM 1 & 2 controls

STEP 3:

 

While holding

 the value encoder. Turn either the PARAM 1 

or PARAM 2 knob to assign a parameter.

OR

STEP 4:

 Turn the newly assigned PARAM knob to adjust your mod 

machine parameter.

The 

PARAM 1 & PARAM 2

 knobs allow you to continuously control any desired parameter of the currently active mod 

machine. This can be very convenient in allowing continuous control over the various mod machines. For example, REGEN 

in a flanger can be assigned to one of the parameter knobs to allow for continuous control over the flanger feedback.
